## Professional Mathematical Tools

Numix is a comprehensive mobile application suite designed for business professionals who need precise mathematical calculations for commercial operations. Built with Flutter and powered by rigorous mathematical precision, Numix provides essential tools for discount management, pricing strategies, inventory tracking, and sales analysis.
